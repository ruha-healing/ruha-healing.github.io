Welcome to Ruha Healing. I’m Elvira and Ruha means Soul in the language of Kundalini Yoga.

Join live session

I'm a Kundalini Yoga teacher who organizes live classes online and onsite as well as for closed corporate groups.

A typical Kundalini Yoga class consists of a small warm-up - often involving Pranayama, a Kriya as the central experience, long deep relaxation and a final meditation. The world of Kundalini Yoga is very colorful and I invite you from the depth of my heart to try it out.

Checkout my YouTube channel and enjoy some free yoga sets.

Next live sessions

🎉 What

We do a 75 minutes Kundalini Yoga set together. You can join from wherever you are. All you need is an internet enabled device such as a laptop, tablet or phone to be able to see and listen to my instructions.

⏱️ When

At the moment, I am taking a BREAK from my Kundalini Yoga live sessions because I am pregnant. However, as soon as I can continue, the calendar with all dates for our next sessions will appear here again and you can register for them.

💻 How

After my break: Pick a session from the calendar and do the registration. Choose Trial session if this is your first time. You'll receive a follow-up email with detailed instructions on how to join the class.

💲 Costs

Your first session is completely free. If you decide to participate in the future again, I kindly ask you to choose one of the other available ticket options after the trial course.
